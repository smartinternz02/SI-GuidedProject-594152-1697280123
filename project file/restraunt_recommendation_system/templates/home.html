In today's era of excessive information, individuals employ diverse approaches to decide on purchases, leisure activities, and even destinations. Recommender systems serve to automate some of these decision-making methods, aiming to offer cost-effective solutions. The objective is to develop a content-based recommender system wherein upon entering a restaurant's name, the system will analyze reviews of similar establishments. Subsequently, it will suggest other restaurants with akin reviews, sorting them based on the highest ratings.
